2 Project Team

Each project that CfA undertakes will usually consist of the following teams and sub-roles. Each Senior role will be responsible for communicating directly with the Executive Team.

Senior roles in CfA projects are also contributors. We adopt the approach that the idea and implementation should idealy come from the same person. This then adds integrity to a project build as the contibutors beliefs and energy are engrained into their contributions to the project.

2.1 Executive Team

  • 1 x Project Leader
  • 1 x Core Team Member/Mentor

2.2 Design Team

  • 2 x Senior Designers
  • 3+ x Design Contributors

2.3 Technical Team

  • 2 x Senior Developers
  • 3+ x Design Contributors

2.4 Marketing Team

  • 1 x Senior Marketer
  • 2 x Marketing Contributors
