h2. Storyteller (Core Team Member)

Storytellers own the communication streams for their local Brigade.

endprologue.

h3. Description

The Storyteller owns the communication streams for their local Brigade. They tell the stories of the Brigade’s impact in their community. This includes writing blog posts, coordinating with local press, connecting with local media, and working with the Code for Australia Communications team to share stories nationally.

The Storyteller’s areas of focus include:
* Ownership of content, marketing, and social media
* Social media including Twitter, Facebook, Tumblr
* Website
* Blog
* Other content (video, photos, etc.)
* Local press

h3. Relevant skills

This role is a great fit for someone with strength in written communication and social media. This person should be comfortable talking to press and representing the Brigade.

h3. Commitment

* Attend monthly Hangout for Storyteller Core Team Members
* Up to five hours per month writing blog posts, updating social media, and connecting with local press
* Attend at least one hack night per month 
* Attend Brigade events 

h3. What Code for Australia Provides

* Training in relevant areas such as outreach/recruiting best practices, orientation and curriculum development, and more
* Forum to connect with Community Organizer Core Team Members in Brigades across the world

h3. Metrics

The Storyteller will track the following:
* Blog posts written
* Press about the Brigade
* Social media
